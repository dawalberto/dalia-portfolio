THE CHALLENGE

Banc Sabadell needed to modernize its digital presence to compete with new fintech players. The goal was to simplify complex banking operations and provide a more user-centric experience, reducing friction in daily tasks.

  • Simplify the onboarding process for new customers.
  • Redesign the transfer and payment flows to be more intuitive.
  • Integrate a personal finance management tool.

BENCHMARK

Analyzed competitors like Revolut, N26, and traditional banks undergoing digital transformation. Key findings included:

  • Minimalist interfaces with clear calls to action perform better.
  • Real-time notifications and spending categorization are highly valued.
  • Biometric login is now a standard expectation.

INFORMATION ARCHITECTURE

Restructured the app's navigation to prioritize the most frequent actions:

  • Dashboard with customizable widgets for quick access.
  • Unified "Move Money" center for all types of transfers.
  • Contextual help and support available from any screen.

FINAL SCREENS & DESIGN SYSTEM

Delivered a complete set of high-fidelity screens and a comprehensive component library.

  • Clean, modern visual style aligned with the brand's new direction.
  • Interactive prototypes for user testing and stakeholder approval.
  • Detailed documentation for the development team.

CONCLUSIONS

  • User satisfaction scores increased by 25% after the redesign.
  • Task completion time for transfers was reduced by 40%.
  • The new design system accelerated future feature development.
